Ceremonial and Burial Hire

Booking Price: From £200


Marking the end of a chapter and celebrating someone's life achievements and successes is always a sensitive time. A bagpiper can take on requests traditional tunes or personal songs that can uplift the spirit or touch the hearts of families and friends. The sound of the bagpipe is the perfect send-off for your loved ones


Parts we recommend playing at:


  • Pipe as the hearse arrives
  • Lead the coffin into the service
  • Pipe during the service
  • Pipe the coffin out of the service
  • Lead the coffin to the hearse or cemetery
  • Pipe the coffin to the graveside
  • Pipe as the coffin is lowered
  • Pipe as the mourners leave
