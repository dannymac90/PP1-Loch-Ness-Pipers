About Us

The Loch Ness Pipers is a well established service to the local wedding industry, in schools and universities, hotels, local business, tourist events, distilleries and much more. We work in partnership with a team of Inverness’ finest pipers, that are hand selected based on their piping ability to ensure we provide a truly professional service that keeps clients coming back to us time and time again. Whatever the event, be it a wedding, funeral, party, Burn's night celebration or gala dinner, the bagpipes always add a touch of style and prestige, leaving your guests with lasting memories. With every detail tailor-made to your own requirements, you can be assured of a professional and personal service.

About our pipers

Thomas MacLeod

Thomas has been playing the pipes for 27 years winning silver and gold medals at the Northern Meetings competition. Thomas has had success both as a solo piper as well as playing with grade 1 bands. Thomas lives to perform and add his special musical touch to events to make lasting memories.

Ethan MacLaren

Ethan is a traditional piper with an extensive repertoire. Ethan has a proven record having performed at over 700 private events ranging from weddings and funerals to Burns Suppers and corporate events and everything in-between. No event is too big or too small for Ethan to perform at.

Greg Cathcart

Greg is a well known, established piper in the Highland music scene. Greg has played as a session piper for many ceilidh bands and has featured on several hit songs including “Blast from the past” by the Sneckies. Whatever the event, you can be sure that Greg will make a lasting memory.

Daniel Rankin

Daniel developed an early interest in the bagpipes starting at just age 4. Daniel started his piping career playing for the Inverness Youth Pipe Band and became the piper major at only 9 years old. Daniel has gone on to win world championships playing with grade 1 Evanton and District Pipe Band.
